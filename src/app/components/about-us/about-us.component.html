ABOUT US Welcome to the ToDo List Application by Yash Bansal, a simple yet powerful task management tool designed to help you streamline your day-to-day activities. Whether you're juggling multiple projects, keeping track of personal tasks, or organizing a to-do list, our app is built with efficiency and ease of use in mind.

Key Features:
Add, Manage, and Organize Tasks:
With a clean and intuitive interface, adding and organizing tasks has never been easier. You can create a new task by providing a title and description, which allows for better clarity and purpose.
Connect with Me:
You can easily mark tasks as done by simply checking the box. This allows you to track your progress and visualize the completion of your to-do list.
Delete Unwanted Tasks:
If a task is no longer relevant or completed, it can be swiftly deleted with a single click, keeping your task list clean and focused.
Responsive Design:
The layout of the app is fully responsive, adapting to different screen sizes and devices, ensuring an optimal user experience whether you're using a desktop, tablet, or smartphone.
Grid View for Task Management:
The app utilizes a grid layout for task organization, offering a clearer, more structured view of your tasks. This grid layout makes it easy to distinguish between multiple tasks and ensures your list stays visually organized, no matter how many items are added.
Local Storage for Data Retention:
Unlike cloud-based apps, our ToDo List stores all your tasks locally on your device. This ensures that your data is private, secure, and available even without an internet connection. Your tasks will remain saved and accessible, giving you peace of mind. Our goal is to provide users with a functional, stylish, and reliable tool for managing everyday tasks. The app's design and performance reflect our commitment to providing an organized solution that meets the needs of busy professionals and everyday users alike.
